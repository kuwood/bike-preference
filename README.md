# Fair Weather Cyclist

## Link:
http://keithunderwood.com/fair-weather-cyclist/

## Summary: 
This app will help you plan round trip travel on bicycle by providing a map with directions along with the weather forecast for the time of travel in each direction.

### Home Page
<img src='https://i.imgur.com/N2Q0mxQ.png' width='800'>


### Setting your route information:
Here you will select your destination as well as the time you will head there, using google places autocomplete and the time picker. You will then choose your starting location which will also be your return destination as well as the time of travel for that leg.
